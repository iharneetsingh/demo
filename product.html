1. 100% oil-free: using the world’s most advanced oil-free water-lubricated screw compressor technology, compared with dry twin-screw, there is no lubricating oil in all parts of the air compressor, ensuring 100% oil-free compression and providing the most reliable Oil compressed air solutions.

2. High reliability: The compressor has few parts and no wearing parts, so it operates reliably and has a long service life.
